Vplates

URL : www.vplates.com.au

Client : Victorian Government

Delivery Company : Friday Media

Brief Summary :
Victorian Government based website for the sale of customised number plates.

Requirements :
To build a user friendly website which would effectively advertise 'Vplates' and guide users through the process creating a customised number plate.

We were required to build a system to manage the configuration options of millions of available combinations across a variety of different styles. With hundreds of thousands of combinations already sold the system had to be fast, engaging and easy to use.

Personal Involvement :

  • Complete flash development of the 'Plates Styles' tool. This tool forms the basis of the entire plate selection process and effectively condenses hundreds of pages of information into a user-friendly interactive process of about 6 steps. The tool allows users to find plates styles which suit their combination, browse plate styles, detect validity of combinations based on selected theme, define plate sizes and colours, search for similar combinations, search the availability of plate combinations, and preview plates on a car/bike. While the tool was primarily built to tie into a 3rd party purchasing platform, it also provided the ability to allow plates to be purchase directly from within the vplates site.
  • Project Management and Development of premiership plates ordering facility. (including payment gateway integration)
  • Project Management and Complete development of gift certificates order facility (including payment gateway integration)
  • Project Management and Complete development of the dealership subdomain ordering system.
  • Project Management and development of the customised plate suggestion tool. Providing vplates with the ability to create images of combinations they would explicitly like to advertise. This tool was again tightly integrated into the plate styles tool.
  • Complete development of Flash banner system on home page which dynamically reacts to the interactions of the user. I also created several sub banners were used in this tool at different times.
