About

I began my career as a web developer 12 years ago, starting off in a small web development company in Melbourne called Friday. I have since moved to Perth and have spent the last 5 years working for a Las Vegas owned mobile game development studio called Tapslots Pty Ltd.

Over my career I have had the opportunity to work with many talented designers, web developers, sales people, project managers, business analysts, seo specialists, infrastructure technicians and computer science engineers.

In addition to working along side such great people I've also been privileged to work for many great clients varying in size across a number of different sectors including government, not for profit, finance, manufacturing, architecture, training and education, travel and tourism and health care.

During my time at Friday Media I've expanded my tool box as a web developer and proven myself as a dedicated team member with an ability to undertake the research, planning, documentation and implementation of online projects of varying complexity.

After moving to Tapslots I focused my skills into the emerging mobile games development market. From here I have been able to significantly expand my skills in applications and software development.

I have a strong passion for interactive and engaging mobile application and web design and enjoy the experience of working with clients to meet their business objectives using many of the technologies available today.
