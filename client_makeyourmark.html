Brief Summary :
A website to raise awareness of the high number of early childhood deaths and to encourage people to sign onto a petition to have the Commonwealth government take further action into helping prevent the high numbers of child mortality.

Requirements :
Friday worked in collaboration with a design studio called Diva Data www.divadata.com.au to produce a content managed website. The website utilised the following features

  • Video Competition. A video competition was created that would allow visitors to the site to upload videos they had created and stored on you tube to the site.
  • Contact us Electronic Petition form
  • Events and news manager. Tying back from events Save the children were organising at various events including Moomba, Byron blues festival and Kiss my grass.
  • Social media integration. Including Facebook, twitter
  • Content managed ordering game (basic ordering game). In which users are asked to rank issues affecting child poverty from highest to lowest.
