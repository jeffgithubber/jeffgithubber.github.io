Brief Summary :
A web site for Ellis Benson - a sole trader specialising in selling customised stationary online.

Requirements :
To build an online system that would allow customers to create and purchase their own personalised stationary.

After selecting from various stationery templates the user is able to view their product as they change the colours, images and text to create their own unique stationery. Once users have created their stationery they are able to add it to their shopping cart and pay for it online.

Due to the initial success of the website, Ellis Benson has been able to extend their online product range to include pens, art work and other products.

Additional changes are made on a regular basis to allow for the sale of new products, revamps of templates and to include gift certificates and discount codes.
