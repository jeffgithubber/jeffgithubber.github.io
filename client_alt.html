Client : Aquaitic Leisure Technologies

Brief Summary :
I have worked at Aquatic Leisure Technologies for 1 year. Aquatic Leisure Technoliges is the biggest fibreglass pool manufacutrer in Australia. They own and manage the following brands; Bucaneer, Sapphire, Aquatechnics, Pool Colour Guard, instyle concrete pools, challenge marine and quickform. During this time I played a crucial role in implementing subversion and git version control sytems into their web development work flow. Over my time I was able to extend the existing template based content management systems that were in place. I was primary responsible for conceptualising, reseasrch and developing the 3d pool models within the sites. I was able to work with the engineers to take the max models, texturise and integrate them into a custom designed ui. During this process I investigated the capabilities of away 3d on iphones. I purchased a developer license and have created test apps on my phone. I also experimented with phone gap. I also developed internal flash apps for generating graphic monthly reports, based on specs provided. I also created html5 games which connect to facebook and take advantage of the box2d physics engine (see example). In my last months with the company I developed an air app which was to be used in a new e store they were creating in Manduarh. This kiosk was built in flex using the cairngorm framework. The app allowed clients to filter and search for pools, view videos and browse galleries.
