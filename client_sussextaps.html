Sussex Taps

URL : www.sussextaps.com.au

Client : Sussex Taps - Company

Delivery Company(s) : Friday Media | Fresh Brand

Brief Summary :
Sussex Taps is an Australian owned company established in 1995 whom designs, manufactures, distributes and supplies solid brass tap ware and bathroom accessories.

Requirements :
To assist in the rebranding of Sussex taps by supplying a cutting-edge, interactive and product-focused website, to better promote Sussex Taps and their products.

The main objectives of the website rebranding were to:

  • Captivate and engage the user
  • Enable the customer to easily browse, compare and view products
  • Allow back end management of product suppliers
  • Enable fine-grained control of products through the backend
  • Include a way for customers to store a list of their favourite products
  • Displaying feature ranges and products
  • To include a Geo-location integration with Google Maps to show where products are located by post code
  • Allow users to browse products by categories, product applications or design ranges.
  • Include a PDF Store for users to download any application documentation attached to products
  • Send to Friend functionality, enabling free promotion of products via email links which are traceable in the CMS backend.

Personal Involvement :

  • All frontend web development including Geo locating
  • Complete development of Supplier Manager
  • Product Manager development
  • Complete development of Send to Friend functionality
