Brief Summary :
Friday Media worked in collaboration with a design and marketing firm called Magnum Opus to create a website called Ranger Roo for Parks Victoria. The main goal of the website was to attract the attention of children aged 6-8 years old and entice them to visit parks throughout Victoria.

Requirements :
Friday worked on the initial creation of the Ranger Roo website back in 2006. The site was originally created as a Content Managed html/flash mash up. An example of which can be found at http://rangerroo.fridaymedia.com.au/.

In 2006 the aim was to create a user interactive CMS website that would engage young children through various games.
In 2011 Parks Victoria requested an update of the site to provide a fresher, brighter feel and allow for a more tailored site to meet their specific yet changing needs.

Children are now encouraged to register as members using the website. Once registered they can upload photos, read mail messages, view a different views of the park they have created and see a personalized version of the website.
